Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

YouTube Automation Desktop (“we”, “our”, “the application”) respects your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The application may collect the following:

  • YouTube OAuth authorization tokens

2. How We Use the Information

Information is used solely to enable YouTube uploads and manage user accounts.

3. Data Storage

No personal data is stored on external servers. All OAuth tokens remain on the user’s local machine.

4. Sharing of Data

We do not sell, share, or transfer your information to third parties.

5. Google API Compliance

This app complies with Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
